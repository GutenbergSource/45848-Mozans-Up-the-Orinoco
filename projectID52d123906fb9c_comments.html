A travelogue relating of a trip through Venezuela and Colombia slightly over 100 years ago.

No particular difficulties. A bunch of Spanish phrases and verse throughout the work, but not too much; just carefully mind the accents.

Due to the quality of the available scans, the letters h/b, c/e, and n/u have sometimes been mixed up. Wordcheck should catch most of these, but be aware of he/be errors.

The original scans are at The Internet Archive, and may be more readable on the pages with plates.

Have fun!
